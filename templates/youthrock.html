The Point 94.1 and Arkansas Symphony Orchestra Present
Paganini, Bruch, and Prokofiev meet Metallica, Led Zeppelin & AC/DC in an exciting partnership between the Arkansas Symphony Orchestra and The Point 94.1! Rock-star violinist Rachel Barton Pine returns to Little Rock to lead a series of masterclasses, rehearsals, and finally, a concert featuring Arkansas youth musicians accompanying the fiery virtuoso.

Email Barbara or call 501-666-1761, ext. 116 for information on participating in the camp.
