Tom McDonald, Academy Orchestra


Tom McDonald has been a violinist in the Arkansas Symphony since 1976 and an orchestra director in the Little Rock Public Schools since 1978. He currently teaches at Central High, Mann Middle School, and Dunbar Middle School. For the past three years he has served on the music faculty at Arkansas Governor.s School. He also serves as Concertmaster of the Pine Bluff Symphony and is a former director of the Pine Bluff Youth Orchestra. Tom has a son Thomas, also a violinist.
