John Jarboe, Prelude Orchestra


John Jarboe holds a Bachelor of Music Education and Master of Music degrees from Pittsburg State University in Pittsburg, Kansas. He has studied violin with Markwood Holmes and Merton Shatzkin while at PSU and Henri Temianka at California State University in Long Beach. Mr. Jarboe has performed in symphonies on the west coast, east coast, the Nashville Symphony, and was, for 15 years, a violinist in the Arkansas Symphony Orchestra. He is a published Nashville songwriter and poet whose poetry appears in the American Suzuki Journal and the American String Teacher. He is currently employed as the Music Specialist/ Program Advisor in the Arkansas Department of Education.
