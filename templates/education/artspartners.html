The Arkansas Symphony Orchestra has been a part of the music education landscape for many years. Over the last ten years, we have been focused on bringing curriculum-based music programs to children throught the state. If the Arkansas Symphony Quartets have visited your school in the past, you are already familiar with the quality programming we provide. If we have never been to your school, you are in for a treat.

Below are our offerings for the 2013-2014 school year, as well as a link to the (Arts in Education) Mini-Grant Application for the Arkansas Arts Council, which many of our schools have found valuable in covering costs. We are presenting four programs this year that we feel offer something valuable to every child from 2nd through 8th grade, are linked to Arkansas Curriculum Guidelines, and are at a cost affordable to every school.
