Orchestra and You


Designed Especially for 1st-3rd Graders

Co-sponsored by the ASO Guild, this FREE program introduces children to all of the instruments in an orchestra. Guild members organize students into an orchestra using plastic instruments, and one lucky student gets to lead peers as the conductor! This program also features a visit by a Symphony musician.

To schedule this program, please contact Jo Sears-Harkins at (501) 425-8964. Available only in Pulaski County.
