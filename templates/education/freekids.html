Entergy Kids' Ticket


The Arkansas Symphony Orchestra presents its newest outreach program to all children and parents in the entire state of Arkansas. This program offers every student, from Kindergarten to High School seniors—all 400,000 of them—the chance to experience live orchestra performances, some of them, perhaps for the first time in their lives.

To take advantage of this special program, call our Box Office at (501) 666-1761 to book your tickets. We apologize that we cannot accept orders for this program online at this time.
