Symphony Designer House


Symphony Designer House XXI, a premier Arkansas event since 1974, will open its doors on April 15 and run until May 8th. This major biennial event is a forum for Arkansas. premier interior and landscape designers. This year's theme is "Rediscovering Home." Over 30 designers will come together to display contemporary and classic artistry in home design. The home will also display local culinary talent, with local celebrity chefs offering delectible meals at Bach's Lunch Tea Room.

There will be a series of special group events which include four evening events; the Crescendo Party, Thirsty Thursday Party, the Young Professionals Party and the Girl's Night-Out Party. We will also have a Mother's Day Tea to be offered in the Bach's Tea Room. The traditional "Bare Bones" party to celebrate the opening of the house will be on February 24th in the evening. During operational hours our Go for Baroque Gift Shop will be open. Proceeds from this event support the Arkansas Symphony Orchestra.

This year, the home - 23 Edgehill Road . showcases the prestigious Edgehill neighborhood. The home will be open for individual tours from 10 am until 3 pm Tuesday through Saturday and Sunday 1pm . 4pm. Group discounts are available for parties of more than 10.
