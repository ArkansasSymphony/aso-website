Trinity Episcopal Cathedral (I.N.C. Series)

Robinson Center Music Hall


The May 3-4 Acxiom Pops Live! Series concert, Shower the People: The Music of James Taylor, will be held at the Robinson Center Music Hall.

426 West Markham, Little Rock, AR 72201

Map and Directions from Google Maps


From Conway
Take I-40 East to I-30 South (cross the Arkansas River).
Exit 141-A - Convention Center. Follow the signs that say Convention Center (traveling west on 2nd Street, 5 blocks to Spring Street and turn right, traveling north to Markham). Robinson Center Music Hall is located at the corner of Broadway & Markham (next door to the DoubleTree Hotel).

From Searcy
Take Hwy 67 South to I-30 South (cross the Arkansas River).
Exit 141-A - Convention Center. Follow the signs that say Convention Center (traveling west on 2nd Street, 5 blocks to Spring Street and turn right, traveling north to Markham). Robinson Center Music Hall is located at the corner of Broadway & Markham (next door to the DoubleTree Hotel).

From Benton
Take I-30 North.
Exit 141-A - Convention Center. Follow the signs that say Convention Center (traveling west on 2nd Street, 5 blocks to Spring Street and turn right, traveling north to Markham). Robinson Center Music Hall is located at the corner of Broadway & Markham (next door to the DoubleTree Hotel).

From Pine Bluff
Take Hwy 65 North to I-30 North.
Exit 141-A - Convention Center. Follow the signs that say Convention Center (traveling west on 2nd Street, 5 blocks to Spring Street and turn right, traveling north to Markham). Robinson Center Music Hall is located at the corner of Broadway & Markham (next door to the DoubleTree Hotel).

From West Little Rock
Take I-630 East.
Exit Broadway and travel north to Markham.
Robinson Center Music Hall is located at the corner of Broadway & Markham (next door to the DoubleTree Hotel).



Clinton Presidential Center

Back to top

River Rhapsodies Chamber Series concerts are held in the Great Hall of the Clinton Presidential Center.

41200 President Clinton Avenue, Little Rock, AR 72201

Map and Directions from Google Maps


From the South
I-30 into Little Rock.
Exit 141A (Clinton Ave.) off I-30 overpass.
The Clinton Center is easily visible east of I-30 at this point. This exit splits. Loop to the right down to the stop sign on 2nd Street.
Go straight one block over on Ferry St. to stop sign.
Turn right on President Clinton Ave. and go straight.

From the Northeast
Coming from Highway 67 and I-40 West, merge left towards Little Rock onto I-30 crossing over the Arkansas River.
Take Exit 141A (Clinton Ave.) off I-30 overpass.
The Clinton Center is easily visible east of I-30 at this point. This exit splits.
Loop to the right down to the stop sign on 2nd Street.
Turn left; then left again on Ferry Street to stop sign.
Turn right on President Clinton Ave and go straight.

From the Northwest
I-40 East and merge right towards Little Rock onto I-30 crossing over the Arkansas River.
Take Exit 141A (Clinton Ave.) off I-30 overpass.
The Clinton Center is easily visible east of I-30 at this point. This exit splits.
Loop to the right down to the stop sign on 2nd Street. Turn left; then left again on Ferry Street to stop sign.
Turn right on President Clinton Ave and go straight.
